SCINENTIFI NAME : ORNITHOPTERA CROESUS | RANGE: MALUKU, INDONESIA

NAMED AFTER ALFRED R. WALLACE, THIS BUTTERFLY’S STRUGGLES WOULD SADDEN THE GREAT NATURALIST.

LOCATED MAINLY IN THE MALUKU ISLANDS, THE LOWLAND AREAS OF FOREST WHERE THE BUTTERFLY RESIDES HAS BEEN GREATLY REDUCED BY LOGGING. THE DEFORESTATION CONTINUES TODAY WITHIN COMMERCIAL CONTEXT.

MOSQUITOS IN THE AREA HAVE ALSO POSED A PROBLEM INDIRECTLY – AS THEY ARE FOUGHT OFF BY INSECTICIDES WHICH ALSO AFFECT THIS BEAUTIFUL BIRDWING.
